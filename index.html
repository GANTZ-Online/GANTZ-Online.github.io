How to Play:

The goal in Black Jack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

  1. The player and the dealer are dealt two cards. The dealer's second card is hidden.
  2. Players take turns deciding to "hit" (receive another card) or "stay" (keep their current hand).
  3. If a player's hand exceeds 21, they bust and lose the round.
  4. Once all players have finished their turns, the dealer reveals their hidden card.
  5. The dealer must hit until their hand reaches at least 17.
  6. The player with a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer wins the round.
  7. A hand totaling 21 with the first two cards (an Ace and a 10-value card) is called a "blackjack" and wins immediately, unless the dealer also has a blackjack, resulting in a tie.
